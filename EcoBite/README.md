# EcoBite: Personal Food Safety Scanner

> **"One smart scan. One safe bite."**

**EcoBite** EcoBite is a smart iOS utility app designed to help users instantly identify hazardous food ingredients.

---

## Core Philosophy

*   **Safe Bite, Better Ecosystem:** Safeguarding every bite to protect your health
*   **Smart Tech, Small Bite:** Breaking down complex and bite-sized nutritional data into instant information
*   **Conscious Eating:** Empowering mindful choices that protect personal well-being
